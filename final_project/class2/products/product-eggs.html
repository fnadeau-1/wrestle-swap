Our flock of 200 hens lives on 5 acres of rotating pasture. They eat a diet of grass, bugs, and organic grain — no antibiotics, ever. The result is a rich, flavorful egg with a deep orange yolk.

Eggs are available year-round. Production naturally slows in winter as hens get less daylight.

  • Available: Year-round
  • Winter supply may be limited
  • Collected fresh daily

Keep refrigerated. Unlike store eggs, our unwashed eggs can actually sit on the counter for a week. Once refrigerated, keep them cold.

  • Refrigerate after purchase
  • Best within 3 weeks
  • Store in original carton
  • Keep away from strong-smelling foods
