Strawberry Jam

Small batch, low-sugar strawberry jam made from local berries. 8 oz jar with no artificial preservatives.

We source our strawberries from a single farm 12 miles away and make jam in small 20-jar batches. Lower sugar lets the real strawberry flavor shine through. No pectin, no preservatives.

Strawberry jam is a summer seasonal item. We make it during peak berry season in June and July.

  • Seasonal: June – August
  • Limited batches per week
  • Made from local berries only

Unopened jars keep in the pantry for up to a year. Once opened, refrigerate and use within 3 weeks.

  • Unopened: cool, dark pantry
  • Opened: refrigerate immediately
  • Use within 3 weeks of opening
  • Check seal before purchasing
