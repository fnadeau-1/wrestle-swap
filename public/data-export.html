Export Your Data

You have the right to receive a copy of all personal data GrappleTrade holds about you, including your profile, order history, listings, and ratings.

How it works: We'll email your data export to your verified email address as a JSON file. Allow a few minutes for delivery. You can request one export per 24 hours.

Delete Your Account

You have the right to request deletion of your account and associated personal data. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Before deleting: Any active orders must be resolved first. Open listings will be removed. Your username will be released. Refunds already issued are not reversed.

Other Rights

Under CCPA (California) and GDPR (EU), you also have the right to:

  • Correct inaccurate data — update your profile in Settings
  • Opt out of interest-based advertising — see our AdChoices page
  • Restrict processing — contact us at privacy@grappletrade.com

Questions? Email privacy@grappletrade.com with subject line "Privacy Request".
