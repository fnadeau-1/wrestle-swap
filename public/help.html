Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase an item? +
To purchase an item, simply click on the listing, review the details, and click "Buy It Now". You'll be prompted to sign in if you haven't already, then proceed to checkout where you can enter your shipping and payment information.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Venmo. All transactions are secure and encrypted for your protection.
How do I list an item for sale? +
Click the "Sell" button in the top navigation, fill out the listing form with your item details, upload photos, set your price, and click "List Item". Your listing will go live immediately and be visible to all buyers.
What fees does Wrestle Swap charge? +
We charge a 10% commission on sold items. There are no listing fees or monthly subscriptions. You only pay when your item sells. Payment processing fees may apply depending on your chosen payment method.
How long does shipping take? +
Shipping times vary by seller location and shipping method. Most items ship within 1-3 business days of purchase. Standard shipping typically takes 3-7 business days, while expedited options are available for faster delivery.
How do I track my order? +
Once your item ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also view tracking information in your account under "My Swaps" > "Purchases". Click on the order to see the current shipping status.
How do I reset my password? +
Click "Sign In" and then "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and we'll send you a link to reset your password. The link expires after 24 hours for security purposes.
Can I delete my account? +
Yes, you can delete your account at any time by going to Account Settings > Privacy > Delete Account. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure to resolve any pending transactions first.
What if the item wasn't as described or didn't show up? +
Please contact our support team immediately via the contact form below or email wrestleswap@gmail.com. We will investigate the issue and work with you to find a resolution, which may include a refund or replacement.
How do I collect money from sales? +
Create a Stripe Express account prompted in the "sell" section, this is a secure way to receive payments directly to your bank account. Once your item sells, the funds will be transferred to your Stripe account minus any applicable fees.
