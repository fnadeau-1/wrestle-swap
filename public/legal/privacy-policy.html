Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

GrappleTrade is a peer-to-peer marketplace for wrestling gear. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and your rights. By using GrappleTrade you agree to the practices described here.

1. Who We Are

GrappleTrade is operated by GrappleTrade HQ, 13 Evans Street, Binghamton, NY 13903. Contact us at grappletrade@gmail.com for any privacy-related questions.

GrappleTrade is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address — collected at sign-in via Google OAuth and used for transactional emails and account management.
  • Username — auto-generated on first sign-in; you can change it in Settings. Your username is your public identity on the platform.
  • Google OAuth profile — when you sign in with Google we receive your email address and profile photo URL. Your real name from Google is never stored or displayed publicly on GrappleTrade.

Transaction and Listing Information

  • Shipping address — collected at checkout (full name, street address, city, state, ZIP code).
  • Order records — include product details, price paid, shipping carrier, tracking number, and transaction status.
  • Listing information — title, description, photos, condition, price, and the seller's ZIP code (used to calculate shipping rates at checkout).

Payment Information

We do not store your card number, bank account details, or any payment credentials. All payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe. When you connect a Stripe Express seller account, Stripe collects and stores your financial and identity verification information directly under their own privacy policy.

Device and Fraud Prevention Data

  • reCAPTCHA Enterprise — all pages use Google reCAPTCHA Enterprise to detect automated abuse. This collects hardware and software information, including device and application data, and sends it to Google for analysis. See Section 5.
  • Sentry (error monitoring) — Sentry.io may receive browser type, page URL, and error stack traces when a technical error occurs on the platform.

Messages

Direct messages sent between buyers and sellers are stored in our database. Messages are visible to both parties and to GrappleTrade administrators for moderation and dispute resolution.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the marketplace — displaying listings, processing orders, calculating shipping rates, and facilitating payments.
  • To send transactional emails — order confirmations, shipping notifications, delivery confirmations, watchlist alerts, and account notices.
  • To prevent fraud and enforce our Terms of Service, including detecting bot activity via reCAPTCHA Enterprise.
  • To resolve disputes and respond to support requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations, including IRS reporting requirements (see Section 6).
  • To send a follow-up email if you start but do not complete a checkout. To opt out of these emails, contact us at grappletrade@gmail.com.

4. Information We Share

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as necessary to operate the platform:

Stripe

Your email address is shared with Stripe to associate payment intents with your account. Sellers provide financial and identity information directly to Stripe to receive payouts. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Shippo

When an order ships, the buyer's full name and shipping address and the seller's ZIP code are transmitted to Shippo to generate a shipping label. See Shippo's Privacy Policy.

Resend

Your email address is shared with our transactional email provider to deliver order and account emails.

Google (reCAPTCHA Enterprise)

We use Google reCAPTCHA Enterprise on all pages to detect abusive traffic. reCAPTCHA collects hardware and software information and sends it to Google for analysis. This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Sentry

Application error monitoring. Sentry may receive browser, device, and page-level data when a technical error occurs.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose personal information if required by law, court order, or valid governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of GrappleTrade, our users, or the public.

5. Cookies and Tracking

GrappleTrade uses Firebase for authentication (session cookies) and Google reCAPTCHA Enterprise for fraud prevention (which may set cookies and collect device signals). We do not use third-party advertising cookies or sell data for advertising purposes.

6. Tax Reporting

Payments are processed through Stripe. If you are a seller, Stripe may be required by the IRS to issue a Form 1099-K reporting your gross sales. Stripe collects your tax identification information during seller onboarding to fulfill this obligation.

7. Data Retention

  • Sold listings — automatically deleted 90 days after the sale.
  • User profiles — retained until you delete your account.
  • Order records — retained for dispute resolution and legal compliance. When an account is deleted, your user ID and shipping address are immediately removed from all associated order records (anonymized); the anonymized order record is retained for business and legal purposes.
  • Messages — retained for 2 years after the last activity in the conversation.
  • Abandoned cart records — automatically purged after 7 days.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account at any time from Settings > Account Actions > Delete Account. This deletes your profile, username reservation, and anonymizes your identity in order records. Accounts with active unfulfilled orders cannot be deleted until those orders are resolved.

Profile Updates

You can update your username and other profile information in Settings at any time.

California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have the right to: know what personal information we collect; request deletion of personal information (subject to exceptions); opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information); and non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights. To exercise your rights, email grappletrade@gmail.com.

Access and Correction

To request access to or correction of personal data we hold about you, email grappletrade@gmail.com.

9. Security

We use SSL/TLS encryption, Firebase App Check, and reCAPTCHA Enterprise to protect your data. No method of internet transmission is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children's Privacy

GrappleTrade is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we learn we have collected personal information from a person under 18, we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For material changes, we will provide at least 14 days' notice by email before the changes take effect. Continued use of GrappleTrade after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
