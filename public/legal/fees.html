Fees & Pricing

Simple, transparent pricing — no hidden costs

Effective Date: June 22, 2026

The short version: GrappleTrade charges sellers a 10% fee on the item sale price only. There are no listing fees, no monthly fees, and no markup on shipping.

2 How the Platform Fee Works

The 10% platform fee is automatically deducted from the item sale price before your payout is calculated. The fee is applied to the item price only — it does not apply to the shipping cost paid by the buyer.

Example: $80 wrestling singlet sale

Item sale price$80.00
Buyer pays shipping$12.00
Platform fee (10% of $80)−$8.00
Seller receives$72.00

Example: $200 headgear + $15 shipping

Item sale price$200.00
Buyer pays shipping$15.00
Platform fee (10% of $200)−$20.00
Seller receives$180.00

3 Shipping Costs

Shipping rates are calculated in real time using Shippo and are based on:

  • The item's weight and dimensions as entered by the seller
  • The distance between seller and buyer zip codes
  • The carrier and service level selected at checkout

GrappleTrade does not add any markup to shipping costs. The buyer pays exactly what the carrier charges.

Sellers purchase a prepaid shipping label through the platform at checkout rates via Shippo. The label cost is covered by the shipping amount the buyer paid at checkout.

4 When Fees Are Charged

The platform fee is only charged on completed transactions — when funds are released to the seller after the buyer confirms delivery. Fees are not charged on:

  • Listed items that don't sell
  • Orders that are cancelled before shipment
  • Orders that are refunded through our Money Back Guarantee

5 Payout Schedule

Once a buyer confirms delivery, funds are released from escrow and transferred to your connected Stripe bank account. Typical timelines:

  • Funds released: Immediately after buyer confirms delivery
  • Stripe transfer: 2–5 business days to your bank account (standard Stripe timing)

If a buyer does not confirm delivery, GrappleTrade may release funds after a reasonable review period. See our Money Back Guarantee for dispute timelines.

6 Fee Changes

GrappleTrade reserves the right to change its fee structure. We will provide at least 14 days' notice of any fee changes by posting an announcement on the platform and, where possible, emailing affected sellers.
