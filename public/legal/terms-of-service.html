5 Payments & Escrow

GrappleTrade operates an escrow model to protect both buyers and sellers:

  • Payment processor: All payments are processed by Stripe. Card data never touches our servers.
  • Escrow hold: When a buyer completes a purchase, funds are held by Stripe on behalf of GrappleTrade until the buyer confirms delivery of the item.
  • Seller payout: Funds are released to the seller's connected Stripe account only after the buyer confirms the item was received as described, or after our dispute resolution process concludes.
  • Platform fee: GrappleTrade charges a 10% fee on the item sale price. There are no listing fees. See our Fees & Pricing page for full details.
  • Refunds: Buyers are protected by our Money Back Guarantee. Refunds are issued by GrappleTrade when a valid claim is approved, not at individual seller discretion.
  • Cancellations: If a seller cancels an order or fails to ship within 10 days, the buyer receives a full refund automatically.

Payment Security: Stripe is a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. While we use reputable payment processors, you are responsible for maintaining the security of your account and monitoring for unauthorized activity.
