Seller Agreement

Terms for selling on GrappleTrade

Effective Date: June 22, 2026

This Seller Agreement governs your use of GrappleTrade as a seller. By listing any item for sale on the platform, you agree to be bound by these terms in addition to our general Terms of Service. If you do not agree, do not list items for sale.

1 Eligibility to Sell

To list items on GrappleTrade you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Have a valid Google account used to authenticate on the platform
  • Complete Stripe Connect onboarding to receive payouts
  • Have a valid US bank account for payout purposes
  • Not be currently suspended or banned from the platform

2 Stripe Connect Requirement

All seller payouts are processed through Stripe Connect. Before receiving any earnings you must complete Stripe's onboarding process, which includes identity verification and bank account linking as required by US financial regulations.

  • You may begin listing and selling immediately after basic Stripe onboarding
  • Full verification (identity + bank account) is required before your first payout
  • Earnings are held securely until verification is complete
  • By connecting a Stripe account, you also agree to Stripe's Connected Account Agreement

3 Platform Fee

GrappleTrade charges a 10% fee on the item sale price for each completed transaction.

  • The fee is deducted from the sale price before payout to the seller
  • The fee does not apply to shipping costs
  • There are no listing fees — you only pay when you sell
  • The fee is non-refundable once a transaction is completed and funds are released

See our Fees & Pricing page for examples and a full breakdown.

4 Escrow & Payouts

GrappleTrade uses an escrow model to protect both buyers and sellers:

  • Funds held: When a buyer completes a purchase, payment is held by Stripe on behalf of GrappleTrade
  • Payout trigger: Funds are released to your Stripe account only after the buyer confirms delivery, or after GrappleTrade resolves a dispute in your favor
  • Payout timing: Once released, Stripe typically transfers funds to your bank account within 2–5 business days depending on your account settings
  • Cancellation: If you cancel an order after payment, the buyer receives a full refund and no payout is made to you

5 Shipping Obligations

As a seller on GrappleTrade you are required to:

  • Ship within 7 days of receiving a confirmed order
  • Use a trackable shipping method and upload tracking information to the platform
  • Package items securely to prevent damage in transit
  • Ship to the address provided by the buyer at checkout

Auto-Cancellation: If tracking information is not uploaded within 10 days of order placement, the order will be automatically cancelled, the buyer will receive a full refund, and you will receive a seller strike.

6 Seller Strike System

GrappleTrade enforces a strike system to maintain marketplace quality:

  • Strike 1: Warning issued; order is cancelled and buyer refunded
  • Strike 2: Second warning; selling privileges may be temporarily restricted
  • Strike 3: Account suspended from selling indefinitely

Strikes are issued for:

  • Failing to ship within 10 days (auto-cancellation trigger)
  • Cancelling an order after payment without valid reason
  • Repeated buyer complaints of items not matching descriptions
  • Violations of our prohibited items policy

Appeals: You may appeal a strike by contacting us at grappletrade@gmail.com within 14 days of the strike being issued.

7 Listing Requirements

All listings must:

  • Accurately describe the item's condition, size, brand, and any defects
  • Include clear, honest photographs of the actual item being sold
  • Be priced in USD
  • Represent items you physically possess and can ship
  • Not duplicate an existing active listing for the same item

Misrepresentation of an item's condition or identity may result in an immediate strike and order cancellation at our discretion.

8 Prohibited Items

You may not list the following on GrappleTrade:

  • Counterfeit, replica, or unauthorized branded goods
  • Stolen property or items obtained illegally
  • Weapons, ammunition, or illegal substances
  • Items that are unsafe or fail basic safety standards for athletic use
  • Items prohibited by applicable federal, state, or local law

GrappleTrade reserves the right to remove any listing at any time and for any reason, including items not on the above list.

9 Suspension & Termination

GrappleTrade reserves the right to suspend or permanently terminate your selling privileges at our sole discretion, including but not limited to violations of this Agreement or the general Terms of Service. Upon suspension:

  • Active listings will be deactivated
  • Pending orders must still be fulfilled or will be cancelled with buyer refunds
  • Any earned, unreleased funds will be held pending resolution of open disputes

10 Taxes

You are solely responsible for determining and fulfilling any tax obligations arising from your sales, including income tax and sales tax where applicable. GrappleTrade does not collect or remit taxes on your behalf except as required by law. Stripe may issue a Form 1099-K if your earnings meet applicable IRS thresholds.

Questions about selling? Contact us at grappletrade@gmail.com
