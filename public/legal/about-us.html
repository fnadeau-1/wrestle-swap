About Us Built By Wrestlers, For Wrestlers

As a lifelong wrestler, I've experienced firsthand how expensive gear can be and how hard it is to find quality equipment at a fair price.

That's why I created this platform.

This is a marketplace built for wrestlers, by wrestlers — a place where athletes can buy and sell used gear directly within the wrestling community. Instead of letting perfectly good shoes, headgear, or singlets sit unused, we give them a second life while helping wrestlers save money and stay competition-ready.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple:

  • Make wrestling more affordable
  • Keep quality gear circulating within the community
  • Support athletes at every level

We believe wrestling isn't just a sport — it's a brotherhood and sisterhood. And this platform is our way of strengthening it.

Whether you're a youth wrestler just starting out, a high school grinder, or a college competitor looking to pass down gear, this marketplace is here for you.
