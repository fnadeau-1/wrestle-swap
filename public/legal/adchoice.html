AdChoices & Interest-Based Advertising

Last Updated: November 29, 2024

Notice: Wrestle Swap may display interest-based advertisements. This page explains how we use advertising, what data is collected, and how you can control your advertising preferences.

1. What is Interest-Based Advertising?

Interest-based advertising (also called "online behavioral advertising" or "targeted advertising") is advertising that is tailored to your likely interests based on information collected about your online activities over time and across different websites and apps.

For example, if you frequently browse wrestling equipment on various websites, you may see ads for wrestling gear when you visit other sites or use apps.

2. How We Use Advertising

Wrestle Swap may display advertisements in the following ways:

  • On our website - We may show ads from third-party advertising networks
  • In promotional emails - We may include sponsored content in our communications
  • On other websites - You may see ads for Wrestle Swap on other websites you visit

Types of Ads We Display

  • Wrestling equipment and gear from our marketplace sellers
  • Related products and services from third-party advertisers
  • Sponsored listings and featured items
  • Community events and wrestling competitions

3. Information Used for Advertising

To show you relevant ads, we and our advertising partners may collect and use:

  • Information about items you view, search for, or purchase
  • Your browsing activity on our website
  • Your general location (city or region)
  • Device information (type, operating system, browser)
  • IP address
  • Cookie and tracking technology data

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share non-identifiable information with advertising partners to show you relevant ads, but we never sell your name, email address, or other personally identifiable information.
