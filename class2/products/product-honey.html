Raw, unfiltered wildflower honey with beautiful clover and lavender notes. Each 16 oz jar is unique to the season.

Our bees forage across 10 acres of wildflowers, clover fields, and lavender patches. The honey is extracted cold and never heated, preserving all the natural enzymes and pollen.

Honey is harvested in late summer and fall. Supply is limited to what our hives produce naturally each year.

  • Harvested: August – October
  • Available year-round while supplies last
  • No two batches taste exactly alike

Store honey at room temperature. It never truly expires — if it crystallizes, just warm the jar gently in warm water.

  • Store at room temperature
  • Keep lid tightly sealed
  • Shelf life: indefinite
  • If crystallized, warm gently in water
