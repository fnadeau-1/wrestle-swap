For over a decade, Fresh Roots Market has been a Saturday morning ritual — a place where neighbors meet farmers, kids try a tomato off the vine, and every dollar stays close to home.

Our Story

It started with one farmer and a folding table.

In the spring of 2012, a small group of local farmers set up a handful of folding tables under the Downtown Pavilion. They had crates of asparagus, fresh eggs, and big plans. What started as a handshake agreement between five families has grown into a thriving weekly market with over 40 vendors.

We've stayed true to the original idea: real food, grown by real people, sold close to where it was harvested. Every vendor at Fresh Roots is within 50 miles of the pavilion, and every Saturday — rain, snow, or shine — we open the doors at 7am sharp.

What We Believe

Three things matter most.

🌻

Real Relationships

You should know who grows your food. Every vendor here is a neighbor, and we're proud of the conversations that happen over a basket of peaches.

🌾

Honest Sourcing

No middlemen. No mystery origin labels. If it's at our market, it was grown, baked, or made within 50 miles. Always.

🌎

Sustainable Practices

We support farms that work with the land, not against it. Reusable bags, compostable packaging, and zero-waste vendor goals.
